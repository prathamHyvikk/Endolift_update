Endolift

Why choose it?

Define your jawline:

shrinks early jowls & under-chin fat.

Tighten lax skin:

1470 nm laser heat contracts collagen instantly and triggers fresh production for months.

One-and-done:

results from a single session—no sutures, no scars.

Minimal downtime:

most clients resume their everyday life the next day.

Natural look:

gradual collagen remodelling avoids the “over-pulled” surgical vibe.

How does it work?

A hair-thin (200-300 µm) optical micro-fibre slides below the dermis through a pin-prick entry point. Pulses of 1470 nm energy are selectively:

1

Liquefy tiny fat pads

along the jawline and under the chin.

2

Heat the deep dermis,

causing collagen fibres to contract instantly.

3

Stimulate fibroblasts,

launching months-long neo-collagenesis that further tightens skin.

The fibre is then withdrawn; the puncture seals without stitches.

What to expect
1

Consultation & mapping

your provider marks treatment vectors.

2

Local anaesthesia

tiny lidocaine bleb; most patients feel only warmth/pressure.

3

Laser pass

fibre is tunneled along the jawline; energy is delivered as the fibre is withdrawn.

4

Post-care

cool compress, OTC analgesic if needed. Avoid vigorous exercise for 48 h.

5

Downtime

The swelling/bruising peaks at 48 h and fades in 5-10 days; makeup is OK after 24 h.
